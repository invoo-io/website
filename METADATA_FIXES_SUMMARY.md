## Technical Notes

### Character Count Guidelines
- **Titles**: Optimal 50-60 chars (Google truncates at ~60-70 chars)
- **Descriptions**: Optimal 150-160 chars (Google truncates at ~155-160 chars)

### Acceptable Overages
Some descriptions are 157-161 chars (slightly over 155) but still under Google's hard truncation limit of ~160 chars. These are acceptable trade-offs for maintaining conversational tone and key messaging.

### SEO Keyword Integration
- **"Verifactu"** now appears in Homepage and Freelancers titles (ES & EN)

- Spanish market focus with "autónomos", "gestoría", "pymes" keywords
