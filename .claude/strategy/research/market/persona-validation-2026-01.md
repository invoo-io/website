## Key Findings Summary

### 1. Verifactu is the Forcing Function (Critical)

- **82% of SMEs** with <50 employees NOT ready for compliance
- **38% admit** they will delay until last minute (risking €50,000 fines)
- **60% view postponement** to 2027 as relief (indicates high stress)
- Word/Excel invoices become **ILLEGAL** for B2B from July 2027

**Quote**: "Muchos autónomos han manifestado su frustración y preocupación ante la llegada de Verifactu"

### 2. Gestoría Market Under Threat

- **82,000+ gestorías** in Spain (€28B+ sector)
- **78.5% have <10 employees** (fragmented market)
- Online competitors pricing **60-70% below** traditional (TaxDown €30 vs €80-150)
- **#1 reason clients leave**: Lack of communication
