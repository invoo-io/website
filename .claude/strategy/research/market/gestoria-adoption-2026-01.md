# Gestoría Adoption Research - January 2026

> **Date**: 2026-01-23
> **Purpose**: Validate whether "gestoría collaboration" positioning is relevant to all autónomos or just a segment
> **Result**: VALIDATED - 90% of autónomos use gestorías

---

## Key Statistics

| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Autónomos using gestorías | **90%** | ATA / Servimedia |
| DIY (completely independent) | **9.4%** | ATA study |
| Trust gestor > Hacienda | **94%** | Professional survey |
| Typical gestoría cost | **€50-150/month** | Multiple sources |

---

## Adoption Breakdown

| Segment | % of Market | Description |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Full gestoría model | ~70% | Outsource all tax/admin to professional |
| Hybrid model | ~25% | Self-invoice but consult gestor quarterly |
| Pure DIY | ~10% | Manage taxes completely independently |

---

## Why Autónomos Choose Gestorías

1. **Bureaucratic complexity** - 60 regulatory changes/month in 2024
2. **Fear of costly mistakes** - Penalties 50-150% of owed amount
3. **Time scarcity** - Want to focus on business, not compliance
4. **Tax optimization** - Identify deductible expenses
5. **Peace of mind** - Professional liability if gestor errs

## Trend: Increasing (Not Declining)

**Growth drivers:**
- Verifactu compliance (mandatory 2027)
- Electronic invoicing requirements
- Real-time VAT reporting
- Digital gestorías lowering barriers
