# Competitor Positioning Analysis - January 2026

## Positioning Summary

| Competitor | Primary Claim | Target | Price | Key Weakness |

| **Holded** | All-in-one business management | SMEs, autónomos | €29-159/mo | Feature bloat, poor support, expensive |

---

## Common Patterns (What Everyone Does)

1. **All-in-one positioning** - Everyone tries to be everything
2. **Verifactu compliance** - Now table stakes, not differentiator
3. **AI/automation claims** - Overused, under-delivered
4. **Poor customer support** - Universal weakness
5. **Hidden costs** - Extra users, support, modules
6. **Freemium or promotional pricing** - 50% off first 3 months
