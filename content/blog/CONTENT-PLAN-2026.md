## Article Details

### 1. Invoo vs Holded

**File:** `content/blog/comparaciones/invoo-vs-holded-comparativa-2026.md`

**Target keyword:** `alternativa a holded`, `invoo vs holded`

**Why now:**
- Holded user complaints surging (support, pricing)
- Moloni shutdown (Dec 2025) driving users to research alternatives
- Zero existing "Invoo vs Holded" content = first-mover advantage
- Q1 = prime decision-making season

**Unique angle:**
- Price advantage (Holded €15-45/mes vs Invoo €10.90/mes)
- Support quality contrast (cite Trustpilot reviews)
- Simplicity for autónomos vs Holded's ERP complexity
- Gestoría collaboration feature (unique to Invoo)

**Key sections:**
1. Tabla comparativa visual (precio, funciones, soporte, Verifactu)
2. Cuándo elegir Holded (honest: ERP needs, inventory, multi-user)
3. Cuándo elegir Invoo (precio, simplicidad, soporte)
4. Opiniones reales de usuarios
5. Guía de migración Holded → Invoo
6. FAQs

**Word count:** 1000-1200
